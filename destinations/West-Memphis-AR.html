West Memphis, Arkansas

Just across the Mississippi from Memphis, West Memphis offers something unexpected for a discerning traveller: gentle river‑view landscapes, tree‑lined suburban roads, and now a quietly momentous transformation in progress. A massive data‑centre campus — developed under the name Groot LLC — has been officially confirmed to be backed by Google.

Imagine a long‑weekend stay where you lodge in a boutique riverside hotel, wander along the levee, enjoy dining in the city’s revitalising downtown, and then step back and notice: a sprawling 1,178‑acre campus is planned just beyond the outskirts, with five data‑centre buildings, internal roads, a massive substation and industrial­‑scale infrastructure creeping into what was once quiet fields.

Here’s the twist: the benefit for a luxury‑traveller is the intersection of ambiance and awareness. The investment being made isn’t just about servers — it includes a $10 billion scale (with potential for even more) and a deep partnership with the local utility Entergy Arkansas to build new battery storage and solar resources.

Stroll the waterfront at dusk. Sip a glass of wine on a terrace. And know this: the next morning you might glimpse construction markers or environmental‑review filings on road signage, a subtle reminder that the future is building itself here. West Memphis offers refinement in the shadows of innovation and invites a quiet reflection—luxury softened by purpose.

Stay – Big Cypress Lodge

Located just across the river in Memphis, this distinctive luxury property blends upscale comfort with Southern charm. From pet‑friendly suites to elevated design features, it’s a refined base for exploring the West Memphis region.

Tip: Choose a deluxe suite with river or skyline views and plan an early evening arrival to enjoy the ambiance before your exploration of the nearby data‑centre site begins.

Dine – Ignite Steakhouse

Just minutes from downtown West Memphis, Ignite Steakhouse offers charcoal‑grilled premium steaks paired with curated wines and artisan cocktails. It’s one of the most refined dining options in the Mid‑South region

Tip: Request the Chef’s Table for a more immersive experience. Pair your entrée with a local vintage or craft cocktail and consider starting your evening here before a stroll by the river.

Relax – Feathers Spa at The Peabody Memphis

For a top‑tier spa experience, Feathers Spa at The Peabody in Memphis delivers luxurious treatments including body wraps, hydrotherapy showers, and full‑scale salon services.

Tip: Book a “sound‑scape massage” or a deep‑relaxation treatment after a day exploring the region where infrastructure and landscape converge. Arrive early to enjoy the lounge amenities, steam rooms and reflect on the dual nature of your surroundings.
