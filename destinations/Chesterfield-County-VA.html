Chesterfield County, Virginia

Nestled just outside Richmond, Chesterfield County feels at first glance like refined suburban Virginia—quiet tree‑lined lanes, historic estates, and rolling green parks. But under the surface lies a cutting‑edge transformation: two major development sites, comprising about 1,200 acres and nearly $60 million in land deals, have been acquired for multi‑billion‑dollar data‑centre campuses.

Imagine staying in a chic countryside inn near Moseley, wandering vineyards in the golden hour, and then recognising that parts of the horizon—fields once destined for homes—are now zoned exclusively for vast server farms. The site near Upper Magnolia Green (Tract A) will be dedicated to data‑centres and related uses, while a second site near Westchester Commons will follow suit.

For the luxury‑traveller who senses the pull of stories beneath the surface, Chesterfield offers a layered experience: stay for world‑class comfort, dine in elegant local restaurants, then consider the scale of what’s happening—facilities that might demand enormous power, water, and infrastructure, wrapped in non‑disclosure agreements and quiet tax incentives.

Whether you're drawn by upscale relaxation or intrigued by the invisible backbone of digital society, plan a couple of nights here. Enjoy refined amenities by day, take a quiet walk among the trees at dusk, and from your terrace consider the hum of the future being built just beyond. Chesterfield: where elegance meets infrastructure, whispering possibilities.

Stay – Boutique Inn or Upscale Hotel

A historic inn‑style B&B just outside Chesterfield offering character, refined décor, and personalised service. It’s ideal for a relaxed, premium stay that contrasts with the tech‑infrastructure theme of your article.

Tip: Choose a “Suite Garden” room for extra space and quiet, then take a leisurely breakfast in their veranda before exploring the county.

Dine – Fine Dining Restaurant

Located within the Highlands Golf Club, The Reserve offers a refined dining experience in Chesterfield County. The space, cuisine and ambiance fit nicely into a luxury‑travel setting.

Tip: Reserve a table at dusk to catch individual course presentations that elevate local ingredients and savour the comfortable setting after a walk around the grounds.

Relax – Wellness / Spa Retreat

Just a short drive from central Chesterfield, this organic spa offers massages, facials and full‑day packages which make a strong fit for a luxury stay oriented around reflection and comfort.

Tip: Book a “Full Day Escape” package: arrive early, start with a facial, then move to a massage, and end with quiet time in their relaxation lounge – perfect to decompress from the day’s exploration.
